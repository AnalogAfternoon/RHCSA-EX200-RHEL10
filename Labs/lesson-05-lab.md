# Lesson 5 Lab — grep and Regular Expressions

Complete the following tasks. Do not refer to your notes.

1. Search `/etc/passwd` for all lines containing the word "root".

2. Search `/etc/passwd` for all lines that do NOT contain "nologin".

3. Count how many lines in `/etc/passwd` contain the word "bash".

4. Search `/etc/passwd` for lines that begin with the word "root".

5. Search `/etc/passwd` case-insensitively for the word "ROOT".

6. Search all files in `/etc` recursively for the word "sshd".

7. Display lines from `/etc/passwd` that contain any digit.

8. Pipe the output of `ls /etc` into grep to show only lines ending in `.conf`.
