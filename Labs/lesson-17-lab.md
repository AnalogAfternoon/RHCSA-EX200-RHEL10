# Lesson 17 Lab — Configuring Logging

Complete the following tasks. Do not refer to your notes.

---

1. View all journal logs from the current boot.

2. View only error-level journal messages from the current boot.

3. View live journal logs as they come in.

4. View journal logs for the `sshd` service only.

5. View journal logs from the last 30 minutes.

6. Manually send a test message to the system log.

7. View the last 20 lines of `/var/log/messages`.

8. Make the systemd journal persistent across reboots.

9. Check the status of the `rsyslog` service.

10. View logs from the previous boot.
